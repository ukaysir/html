현재 상태

공식 페이지 메타데이터는 확보했지만 첨부 원문 텍스트 추출은 후속 작업이다.

  1. MCST-selected cultural tourism festivals should be treated as a benchmark cohort, not as automatic success proof.
  2. Compare selected/reselected/new festival cohorts against MCST master fields: budget, visitor history, outsourcing, dates, region, festival type.
  3. Use selection/reselection status as one dependent variable in success analysis; use price/crowding/procurement incidents as counter-evidence.
  4. Global festival policy should be checked against demand dispersion: whether national attention spreads to regions or concentrates congestion in already-famous festivals.
