# Budget waste causality model wave 1

Generated: 2026-07-08T04:45:00+09:00

## Core judgment

Do not equate a high festival budget with waste.

Budget-waste risk requires a mismatch between:

- public cost and net cost,
- visitor and satisfaction evidence,
- local economic capture,
- traffic/safety/resident externalities,
- contract scope and accountable operator,
- counter-evidence.

## Official source backbone

- Local Finance 365 event/festival cost accounting.
- Local Finance 365 event/festival budget-line details.
- Contract/procurement notices and contract results.

## Why added now

Recent dossiers created strong price, crowd and mixed-outcome evidence. This model defines how those symptoms should connect to budget-waste claims without overclaiming.
