# Sejong Nakhwa Festival crowd/traffic stabilization dossier

Observed at: 2026-07-08

## Verdict

Sejong Nakhwa Festival is a crowd/traffic stabilization case. The first-year spectacle created a strong visitor draw but exposed parking and traffic bottlenecks. Later years show a public operating response: traffic-control meetings, parking-lot management, illegal-parking enforcement, public-transport promotion, safety/fire-prevention controls, venue/production adjustment and post-event evaluation.

## Guardrail

Do not call this a contractor failure. The public chain points to capacity planning, event-site geometry, public safety/traffic coordination and content/food/facility improvement gaps.
