## Why this exists

The user asked for "why" rather than just a festival list. This wave focuses on repeated root-cause paths:

- Price and booth governance: high price complaints often point to booth selection, external merchants, price/weight disclosure, booth fees and monitoring.
- Demand forecast and safety thresholds: small or mid-sized events can become unsafe when actual demand exceeds the threshold used for planning.
- Budget waste traceability: budget claims need Local Finance 365 cost and budget-line joins before making a causal judgment.

## Guardrail

This queue must not be read as "these festivals failed because of a subcontractor." It says where to look next: booth recruitment, vendor rules, operating agency contract, price disclosure, safety plan, traffic plan, settlement and evaluation records.
