# Jeju Fire Festival content/social-license transition dossier

Observed at: 2026-07-08

## Verdict

Jeju Fire Festival is a content-identity and social-license transition case. Its historic core content, burning Saebyeol Oreum, became the main risk because climate crisis, wildfire risk, ecological damage, legal questions and civic deliberation changed what the public would accept.

## Why this matters

- This is not a simple demand-forecast failure and not a proven contractor failure.
- The same feature that once differentiated the festival became a safety/environment legitimacy problem.
- The festival then entered a redesign loop: 2023 deliberation, 2024 no festival / content development, 2025 virtual fire/media-art transition interrupted by strong wind, 2026 hybrid return with digital fire, torch parade, daljip burning, multi-use containers and price/market controls.

## Guardrail

Keep the causal finding at the governance/content/social-license level. Public sources do not support blaming an agency or subcontractor.
