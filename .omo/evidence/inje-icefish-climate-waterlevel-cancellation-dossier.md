# Inje Icefish Festival climate/water-level cancellation dossier

Observed at: 2026-07-08

## Verdict

Inje Icefish Festival is a climate/water-level cancellation case. The cause is not demand failure or contractor failure. The public evidence points to site-forming conditions: Soyang Dam water level, autumn rain inflow, insufficient freezing, submerged festival area and safety-accident risk.

## Why this matters

It shows that a festival can have strong historical demand and economic effect but still become non-executable when the physical site cannot be safely formed. This is a key comparator against Hwacheon and Cheorwon winter-festival recovery cases.
