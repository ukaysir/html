# Namwon Chunhyang Festival price recovery dossier

Observed at: 2026-07-08

## Verdict

Namwon Chunhyang Festival is a price-governance recovery case. The causal sequence is public and practical: 2023 online price/portion outrage, 2024 direct booth management and strict menu/weight labeling, Theborn Korea/Baek Jong-won food-zone cooperation, price caps, violation expulsion/criminal complaint rules, outsider/illegal short-term merchant enforcement, then 2025-2026 visitor and sales recovery leads.

## Guardrail

Do not merge the KRW 250m promotion/marketing agency tender with the food-price cause. It proves procurement context, not food-booth culpability. Food governance is mainly city/merchant/booth/illegal-outsider control unless a food-zone contract or booth allocation record connects another actor.
