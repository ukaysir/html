# Bonghwa Sweetfish budget/accounting gap dossier evidence note

Scope: public-source-only dossier for Bonghwa Sweetfish Festival / 봉화은어축제, 2016-2026.

Working verdict: budget-accounting gap and natural-shock case, not a proven festival failure. The festival has strong experiential content, paid sweetfish-catching programs, local-voucher refund mechanics, awards and donation signals. The risk is that 2024 fiscal comparison reporting shows high execution cost and 0 revenue despite paid programs, suggesting either accounting/reporting mismatch or weak direct-revenue disclosure. 2023 cancellation was caused by flood damage, not contractor failure.

Core evidence:
- KTO Visit Korea official festival page: 2026 dates, free admission with paid experience programs, KRW 12,000 sweetfish catching fees with KRW 5,000 Bonghwa Love gift-certificate refund, operator Bonghwa Festival Tourism Foundation, climate-response facilities, safety/anti-gouging campaign.
- Bonghwa County official tourism page: festival origin in 1999, clean Naeseongcheon ecosystem, sweetfish catching as core content, resident participation and national reputation.
- Yeongnam/Daum fiscal investigation: 2024 reported execution KRW 2.15067b, settlement revenue 0, economic effect KRW 13.3b with identical plan/result indicator, county comment that internal input and Local Finance 365 display may differ.
- NewsPim/NewDaily 2025: foundation donated KRW 50m from festival revenue to five wildfire-hit cities/counties; shows existence of festival revenue/public-return signal, but not a full fiscal reconciliation.
- KTO/official foundation procurement pages: 2026 event agency RFP/tender and 2026 booth, food truck, bandu-rental recruitment/auction notices exist.
- G2B PDF: 2026 event agency service budget KRW 690m, limited competitive tender, negotiated contract, foundation handles proposal/evaluation/negotiation.
- 2023 flood cancellation sources: prolonged heavy rain flooded Naeseongcheon and caused human/property damage; festival cancelled after emergency board meeting. Classify as natural disaster and safety recovery priority, not demand forecast or subcontractor failure.

Causal guardrail: high public spend + 0 reported revenue is an audit lead, not proof of waste. Need Local Finance 365 raw row, settlement/revenue ledgers, paid ticket sales, gift-certificate redemption and agency scope before assigning waste or contractor causality.
