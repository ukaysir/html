# Sangju World Hat free-access budget-risk dossier evidence note

Scope: public-source-only dossier for Sangju World Hat Festival / 상주세계모자페스티벌, 2023-2025.

Working verdict: free-access budget-risk and content-maturation case, not a confirmed failure. The strongest "why" lead is that 2024 used KRW 1.27134b in city budget with reported admission/business revenue of KRW 0 because all 120,000 visitors entered free; a city official stated paid content was not yet strong or unique enough to charge admission or build a payback cycle. That is an explicit causal explanation for direct-revenue weakness.

Core evidence:
- Official festival page: 2025 festival dates 2025-09-26 to 2025-09-28, venue Taepyeongseongdae Gyeongsang Gamyeong Park, host Sangju City, organizers Sangju Festival Promotion Committee and Korea Hanbok Promotion Institute.
- Yeongnam/Daum fiscal comparison: 2024 Sangju World Hat Festival used KRW 1.27134b entirely from city funds, business/admission revenue KRW 0, all 120,000 visitors entered free; official comment attributes this to insufficient paid/original experiential content and absence of a local-currency payback structure.
- 2023 Kbsm reporter note: early governance/identity confusion around whether the city representative festival was World Hat Festival, budget/department transfer and Korea Hanbok Promotion Institute relationship. Treat as B/C governance-risk commentary, not final fact alone.
- Yonhap 2024: concrete program mix existed: world traditional hat exhibition, fashion show, academic seminar, hat dance competition, 40+ countries exhibition, 8 diplomatic delegations, citizen parade, local arts, hat-making experience.
- 2025 D-100/program sources: FUN FUN HAT theme, Modori game, Hat Experience Big5, 100+ sales/promotion booths, idea/design contests, and national-scale participation expansion.
- Daum/Kyongbuk 2025 interview: 130,000 visitors, average stay 4.8h vs 3.5h, outside visitor ratio 28%, direct economic effect KRW 4.4b, production inducement KRW 7.0b, value-added KRW 2.7b. Treat as reported evaluation/municipal interview lead until evaluation report is obtained.

Causal guardrail: free admission and 0 direct revenue are not automatically waste when public value, identity-building, local sales and direct economic effects are real. But they are budget-risk if high city funding lacks verified local spend, satisfaction, repeat visits, paid conversion or sponsorship/booth revenue.
