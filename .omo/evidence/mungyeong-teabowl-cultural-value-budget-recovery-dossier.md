# Mungyeong Teabowl cultural-value / budget-recovery dossier evidence note

Scope: public-source-only dossier for Mungyeong Teabowl Festival / 문경찻사발축제, 2024-2026.

Working verdict: cultural public-value and budget-recovery gap case, not a proven failure. The festival has strong heritage content, free entry with paid experiences, cultural-tourism status and local tourism spillover. The budget risk is low direct recovery: 2024 reported total cost KRW 1.5b, admission revenue KRW 99.31m, recovery 6.6%, while reported visitors and local economic effect were high.

Core evidence:
- KTO Visit Korea official festival page: 2026 dates, venue, free admission with separate experience fees, operator Mungyeong Teabowl Festival Committee / Mungyeong Tourism Organization, MZ/family content, exhibitions, teabowl making, tea ceremony, escape mission, food-price listing.
- Yeongnam/Daum fiscal comparison: 2024 total cost KRW 1.5b, direct admission revenue KRW 99.31m, 6.6% recovery rate, 280,000 visitors and KRW 16.2b regional economic effect lead.
- Daum/Kyongbuk 2025 wrap-up: 240,000 visitors, schedule moved into May golden holidays, festival pass reworked with Mungyeong Love gift certificate and local tourism discount, outdoor escape room, kiln/studio tour, family programs, KTX Mungyeong Station and free city bus linkage, rest/direction improvements.
- Daum/Daegu Ilbo 2026 preview: honorary cultural tourism festival, international exchange exhibition, MZ/family experiential programs, smart reservation kiosk, road signs, rain/safety preparation and rest/food-truck/shade improvements.
- Mungyeong Tourism Corporation bid pages: 2025 planning/program operation agency RFP posted by Mungyeong Tourism Organization; third-party bid page shows KRW 530m budget and performance requirements; 2024 opening/closing ceremony service was KRW 180m.

Causal guardrail: low direct recovery is a budget-risk lead, not proof of waste. Cultural heritage festivals may justify subsidy through public value, artist ecosystem, tourism spillover and local commerce, but those claims require evaluation method, spend evidence and satisfaction data. Agency responsibility requires contract scope + evaluation + outcome linkage.
