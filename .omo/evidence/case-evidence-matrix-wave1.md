# Case evidence matrix wave 1

Created: 2026-07-08T03:00:00+09:00

Purpose:
- Make the dossier set readable as a decision map.
- Show which cases support contractor/procurement analysis, which support demand/crowding analysis, which are safety/weather cases, and which are pre-event monitoring cases.

Included cases:
- Incheon Pentaport 2019 failure/risk causal candidate.
- Incheon Pentaport 2025 success outcome.
- Bucheon International Comics Festival 2025 governance/outcome bridge.
- Suwon Hwaseong Cultural Festival 2026 pre-event governance/crowd-risk bridge.
- Cheorwon Hantangang Ice Trekking 2026 safety/outcome bridge.

Interpretation:
- Level A: can be analyzed as a causal candidate now.
- Level B+: official governance/procurement chain plus outcome or pre-event risk signal.
- Level C: success/outcome is strong but contractor attribution is not proven.
