# Budget recovery comparator wave 1

Generated: 2026-07-08.

Purpose: turn "high budget means waste" into a better investigation queue. The comparator uses public cost-accounting logic and a media fiscal-analysis lead to separate high-spend/high-recovery festivals from high-spend/low-recovery or zero-revenue cases.

Guardrail:
- This is not a final audit result.
- The numeric rows from the Yeongnam/Daum article are treated as C-level leads until the matching Local Finance 365 event/festival cost-accounting rows and official evaluation reports are joined.
- Recovery rate alone is not the goal; some public-interest festivals can be intentionally free. But low recovery plus weak local-capture evidence is a stronger waste-risk lead than high gross budget alone.
